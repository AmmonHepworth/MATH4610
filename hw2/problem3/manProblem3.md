# Part a: manipulation of the approximation

\[\frac{\sqrt{x+h}-\sqrt{x}}{h} = \frac{\sqrt{x+h}-\sqrt{x}}{h} * \frac{\sqrt{x+h}+\sqrt{x}}{\sqrt{x+h}+\sqrt{x}}
= \frac{x+h-x}{h*(\sqrt{x+h}+\sqrt{x})} = \frac{h}{h*(\sqrt{x+h}+\sqrt{x})} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x+h}+\sqrt{x}} \]
